Ti=Definition Sections

Note=Definition sections are among the most extensively developed parts of the corpus. The Agt-NDA has 16 defined terms with individual files for each. The NVCA and ABA-MSPA have the most extensive M&A-style definition sets. The ANG-Ontology project approaches definitions from an ontological perspective.
