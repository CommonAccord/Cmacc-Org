Ti=Limitation of Liability Clauses

Note=Limitation of liability clauses cap or exclude consequential damages. They are ubiquitous in software, SaaS, data, and services agreements. Variation is significant: some cap at fees paid, others exclude all consequential damages, others are mutual.
