The ContractForTheWeb.org is an initiative of the World Wide Web Foundation to protect and improve the internet as a human resource.
Here we have the basic Principles expressed as Prose Objects. They are different than a "Data Sharing" agreement, but have some similarities with sharing arrangements such as the Chatham House principles for NGOs or the Global Alliance for Genomics and Health's Data Sharing and Data Security Principles.
Somewhat abusively, this is therefore included in the list of S/Index/DataSharing/Frameworks.md
The goal of this is to suggest synergies between these Principles and the other work on Prose Objects, both with respect to the legal aspects and to the Semantic Web expression.
