Based on the draft Consumer Privacy Bill of Rights, this establishes the chain:
CONSUMER <<<- Privacy Policy <<- CPBR <- EXPERTS and STAKEHOLDERS
Iteration will establish:
CONSUMER ->>> Privacy Policy ->> CPBR -> EXPERTS and STAKEHOLDERS
Interactive, distributed regulation.
It will also involve tying this in with requirements of other countries, US States, and specified industries. Anything that affects what a company can or must do with information must be expressed for the company's own purposes their relationships. It should be crowd-source, from experts and stakeholders such as regulators.
